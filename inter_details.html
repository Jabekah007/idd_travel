OUR INTERNATIONAL TOUR


1. Majestic Tanzania Explorer(5days/4nights)

Immerse yourself in the enchanting landscapes of Tanzania, from the vast Serengeti Plains to the pristine beaches of Zanzibar. Witness the breathtaking wildlife of the Ngorongoro Crater and experience the rich culture of the Maasai people.


activities includes:

  • Serengeti National Park Safari, including the Great Migration
  • Ngorongoro Crater Excursion with wildlife viewing
  • Cultural visit to a Maasai village
  • Relaxation on the beaches of Zanzibar
  • Stone Town historical tour
  • Stone Town historical tour
  • Snorkeling and water activities in the Indian Ocean

2. South African Safari Extravaganza (5days/4nights):


Embark on an exhilarating safari adventure in South Africa. Encounter the "Big Five" in their natural habitats, explore the vibrant city of Cape Town, and indulge in the world-renowned wines of Stellenbosch.

activities:

  • Kruger National Park Safari with "Big Five" encounters
  • Cape Peninsula tour, including Cape Point and the Cape of Good Hope
  • Robben Island visit in Cape Town
  • Wine tasting in Stellenbosch and Franschhoek
  • Table Mountain Cable Car experience
  • Cultural tour of Soweto in Johannesburg
  • Hot air balloon safari over Pilanesberg National Park


3. Ivory Coast Treasures (5days/4nights):


Uncover the hidden gems of Cote d'Ivoire, from the historic streets of Grand-Bassam to the lush landscapes of Tai National Park. Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture and sample the exquisite local cuisine.

activities:

  • Grand-Bassam historical walking tour
  • Visit to the Basilica of Our Lady of Peace in Yamoussoukro
  • Tai National Park exploration and wildlife spotting
  • Admire the UNESCO-listed Comoe National Park
  • Yamoussoukro city tour, including the Presidential Palace
  • Experience local cuisine and dance performances


4. Dazzling Dubai Delights(5days/4nights)


Experience the opulence of Dubai with our exclusive tour. Marvel at the iconic Burj Khalifa, shop in luxury malls, and take a desert safari for an unforgettable adventure in the Arabian desert.

activities:

  • Burj Khalifa observation deck visit
  • Desert Safari with dune bashing and camel riding
  • Traditional Dubai Creek Dhow Cruise dinner
  • Explore the Dubai Mall and enjoy the dancing fountains
  • Visit the Palm Jumeirah and the Atlantis Resort
  • Gold and Spice Souk shopping experience
  • Cultural Heritage Tour in Al Fahidi Historic District


5. Singapore Serenity Retreat (5days/4nights):


Discover the perfect blend of tradition and modernity in Singapore. Explore the lush gardens of Gardens by the Bay, savor the diverse culinary delights, and indulge in world-class shopping on Orchard Road.

activities:

  • Gardens by the Bay and Cloud Forest exploration
  • Marina Bay Sands Skypark panoramic views
  • Sentosa Island day trip with Universal Studios or S.E.A. Aquarium
  • Orchard Road luxury shopping spree
  • Chinatown and Little India cultural tour
  • Singapore River Cruise
  • Night Safari at Singapore Zoo

6. Mystical China Odyssey (5days/4nights):


Embark on a journey through ancient and modern China. Visit the Great Wall, explore the Forbidden City in Beijing, cruise the Yangtze River, and witness the vibrant skyline of Shanghai.

activities:

  • Great Wall of China hiking and exploration
  • Forbidden City and Tiananmen Square tour in Beijing
  • Li River Cruise in Guilin with stunning karst landscapes
  • Terracotta Army visit in Xi'an
  • Three Gorges Dam Cruise on the Yangtze River
  • Shanghai city skyline and The Bund exploration
  • Traditional tea ceremony and calligraphy workshop
